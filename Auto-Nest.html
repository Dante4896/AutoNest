Why this mix works

Cheap to make, easy to deliver, simple to test.

You do not need advanced coding or daily customer handling. Most delivery can be automatic through a digital checkout link and download page.

Once one item starts selling, bundle it with related low-ticket add-ons so average order value rises without more manual effort.

How the automation works

The platform is built like a funnel: people discover a useful product, pay once, receive it instantly, and then get automated upsell offers.

Step 1

Traffic

  • Use short-form content and searchable posts.
  • Drive clicks to one main landing page.
Step 2

Capture

  • Offer a free sample.
  • Collect email before or after purchase.
Step 3

Delivery

  • Customer receives files instantly.
  • No manual sending needed.
Step 4

Upsell

  • Auto-email related bundles.
  • Offer higher-value packs later.
